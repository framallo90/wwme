# Professional Sources Used

## 1) UNC Writing Center - Revising Drafts
- URL: https://writingcenter.unc.edu/tips-and-tools/revising-drafts/
- Why it matters:
  - Supports revision order: start with argument/structure, then local edits.
  - Maps directly to pass-based editorial workflow.

## 2) Purdue OWL - Revising Drafts
- URL: https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/general_writing/the_writing_process/revising_drafts/index.html
- Why it matters:
  - Reinforces purpose-first revision and iterative quality checks.

## 3) Real Academia Espanola (RAE)
- URL: https://www.rae.es/
- Why it matters:
  - Authoritative lexical and orthographic reference for Spanish.
  - Supports locale-accurate wording and punctuation decisions.

## 4) Diccionario de la lengua espanola (DLE)
- URL: https://dle.rae.es/
- Why it matters:
  - Practical reference for meaning and accepted word forms.
  - Reduces ambiguity in regional wording decisions.

## 5) PlainLanguage.gov - Guidelines
- URL: https://www.plainlanguage.gov/guidelines/
- Why it matters:
  - Provides professional clarity principles useful for line editing and reader comprehension.
